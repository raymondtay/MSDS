This repository is to create and reinforce the understanding of Probability Theory
that covers the following subjects:

* Descriptive Statistics and the Axioms of Probability
* Conditional Probability
* Discrete Random Variables
* Continuous Random Variables
* Joint Distributions and Covariance
* Covariance and Correlation
* Expectations and Variance
* Central Limit Theorem

The lecture notes are the files in PDF format.
The folder, exercises, contains compartmentalized learnings in the programming
language R.
